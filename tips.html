Always Wear A Helmet

Safety comes first. A good-fitting helmet protects your head and can prevent serious injuries in case of a fall or crash.

Keep your bike maintained

Regularly check your brakes, tire pressure, and chain. A well-maintained bike rides smoother and lasts longer.

Stay Hydrated!

Always bring water, especially on longer rides. Staying hydrated keeps your body energized and prevents fatigue.

Learn road rules & Signals

Know the traffic laws in your area and use hand signals to let others know your next move. This keeps everyone safer.

Dress for comfort

Wear clothing that fits well and suits the weather. Padded shorts help with long rides, and bright colors make you more visible.
